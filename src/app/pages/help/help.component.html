1. Overview

Welcome to Roma, the Romani Morpho-Syntax Database (RMS). This digital platform provides access to a comprehensive collection of Romani dialect samples, linguistic data, and audio recordings for academic research and language study.

2. Search Feature

Purpose: Explore linguistic patterns across different Romani dialects.

How to use:

  1. First select samples from various geographic regions
  2. Choose specific morpho-syntactic categories to analyze
  3. Review the search results showing relevant linguistic data from your selected samples

3. Samples Browser

Purpose: Discover Romani dialects from different locations worldwide.

Features:

  • Geographic information displayed on interactive maps
  • Understanding of geographical distribution of Romani communities
  • Linguistic variations across different regions

4. Audio Phrases

Purpose: Access recorded audio examples in various Romani dialects.

Features:

  • Thousands of recorded audio examples
  • English and other translations provided
  • Play button to listen to native speakers
  • Study pronunciation patterns across different regions

5. Convenience Tables

Purpose: Access structured linguistic data organized by category.

How to use:

  1. Select a sample from the available options
  2. Browse through various table templates
  3. View organized linguistic patterns and comparisons

6. Support Information

Development Team: Roma is developed by the Academy of Sciences of Austria and supported by researchers including Yaron Matras, Viktor Elšík, and Christopher White.
