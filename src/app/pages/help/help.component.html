Help

Welcome to Roma, the Romani Morpho-Syntax Database (RMS). This digital platform provides access to a comprehensive collection of Romani dialect samples, linguistic data, and audio recordings for academic research and language study.

Use the Search feature to explore linguistic patterns across different Romani dialects. First select samples from various geographic regions, then choose specific morpho-syntactic categories to analyze. The search results will show you relevant linguistic data from your selected samples.

Browse Samples to discover Romani dialects from different locations worldwide. Each sample includes geographic information displayed on interactive maps, helping you understand the geographical distribution of Romani communities and their linguistic variations.

The Phrases section contains thousands of recorded audio examples in various Romani dialects, complete with English and other translations. Click the play button to listen to native speakers and study pronunciation patterns across different regions.

Roma is developed by the Academy of Sciences of Austria and supported by researchers including Yaron Matras, Viktor Elšík, and Christopher White. A complete user reference guide will be developed once the platform is fully implemented.
