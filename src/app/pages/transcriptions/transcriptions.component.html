About the Connected Speech Module

The connected speech module provides access to linguistic text transcriptions from the RMS corpus.

  • Speech linked to raw sound data
  • Full text search functionality across all samples
  • Find specific words, phrases, or grammatical forms
  • Search capabilities across the entire corpus (soon)
  • Please note that some connected speech items do not have explaining glosses.
